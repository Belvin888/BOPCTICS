Belvin Kurian

It all started in 2019, where Belvin and Rohit were presented with the task to make a mini project for the year. Through many brainstorming sessions and late night calls, they reached the idea of helping the blind, by making Smart Blind Stick, that could percieve depth and know it's surroundings to alert it's users. Inspite of the pandemic and constraints of money, they worked their way of making a smart blind stick and the rest is history.

Product

Smart Stick With the advent of the first smartphone, things around us are rapidly getting smart. We, while working on this project thought of helping the specially-abled and focused on the fields that haven’t seen much innovations in the smart revolution and chose the blind. The blind, have been using the same stick for millennials and so we envisioned for them and named our prototype the smart stick. The rod used is made of PLA plastics giving it adequate strength without compromising on heft. Under the hood, the brain of the whole operation is a low powered Arduino UNO chip which was programmed to work with our custom tracking module and ultrasonic depth sensors. These collected information are being continuously fed into the mobile application built using the JavaScript. The app enables the user to connect and manage one or many such blind sticks, set perimeters for the distance travelled by the stick, real time location tracking of the stick and to access logs of previous locations recorded by the tracking module making full use of our custom tracking module. The Ultrasonic depth sensors alert the user on upcoming obstacle thereby preventing accidents.
